ThermoSphere transform spaces into environments of pure comfort for anyone to enjoy. Driven by conversations with customers, installers and homeowners, our electric underfloor heating solutions are beautifully simple to specify, install and use.

Fuelled by enthusiasm and the love for what we do, we create an atmosphere where we help our partners to grow, we provide unconditional support for installers, we bring the right solutions to projects and we encourage our people to be the best they can be.

Our promise

We’ll give you the control to create an atmosphere that will transform your house in to a home. Heating is the last thing you should think about and the first thing you should feel.

Use our efficient heating solutions paired with our intelligent thermostats to create a personalised heating system to suit you and your environment, reducing energy wastage and saving you money.

Solutions driven

Being a solutions provider is what motivates us every day. We empathise with our customers and are dedicated to going the extra mile to understand their needs in order to be able to provide reliable practical solutions. We manufacture electric underfloor heating to deliver comfort, safety, health and well-being for you and yours.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in the industry and follow a philosophy of continuous improvement in all areas of business. Everything we do is thoughtfully designed, flawlessly executed, simple to use, and 100% reliable.


Commitment

Our commitment is to be honest, open and direct with our customers, suppliers and team members. By forming long lasting relationships with all of our stakeholders we have built a reputation for reliability which demonstrates we can be trusted to do everything we say we will. We believe in the bold pursuit of excellence with integrity, delivered through teamwork, accountability – and a sense of FUN!
